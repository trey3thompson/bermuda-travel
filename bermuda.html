Attractions / What to Do

  • Horseshoe Bay

    • Discover the allure of Horseshoe Bay in Bermuda, where pink sands meet azure
    waters in a stunning horseshoe-shaped embrace. Bask in the sun, explore hidden
    coves, and savor the breathtaking beauty of one of the island's most iconic beaches.


  • Tucker's Point Golf Course

    • Tee off in paradise at Tucker's Point Golf Course in Bermuda. Nestled amid
    lush landscapes and coastal vistas, this premier golf destination offers an
    unforgettable blend of challenging play and breathtaking views, making it a
    must-experience for golf enthusiasts.

  • Crystal and Fantasy Caves

    • Embark on an underground adventure at Crystal and Fantasy Caves in Bermuda.
    Marvel at the mesmerizing crystal formations and otherworldly landscapes as
    you explore these enchanting caves, offering a captivating blend of natural
    wonders and geological marvels for an unforgettable experience.

    Climate

    Bermuda boasts a subtropical climate that sets it apart from its Caribbean counterparts, offering a unique blend of warmth and mildness. Situated in the North Atlantic, Bermuda enjoys cooler temperatures than typical Caribbean islands, providing a welcome respite from scorching heat. With average temperatures ranging from the mid-60s to the mid-80s Fahrenheit (18-29°C), Bermuda's climate is characterized by balmy breezes and a temperate environment. This makes it an ideal destination for those seeking a tropical paradise without the extreme heat associated with some other island destinations. Visitors can revel in the island's lush greenery, vibrant flora, and pristine beaches year-round, creating a haven for relaxation and outdoor exploration amidst the refreshing Atlantic breezes.
